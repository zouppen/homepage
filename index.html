About

Koodilehto is a cooperative of software professionals located in the Jyväskylä region. We share a common background at the academia. Koodilehto provides the infrastructure for effective collaboration and software development work.

Some of us operate only during spare time while some focus primarily on customer projects. Our greatest strength lies within varied technical expertise.

We develop and demonstrate this expertise actively by authoring, maintaining and contributing to various open source projects. In addition, many of our members have their own personal software projects out there.
